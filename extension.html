How it works
Three steps, zero friction
1

Install the extension

Add Miles Optimizer to Chrome from the Chrome Web Store. It takes about 10 seconds and requires no account or sign-up.

2

Shop as usual

Browse any website normally. When you land on a Flying Blue partner page, the extension icon activates and shows you the current miles rate in a small popup.

3

Earn miles automatically

Click the activation link in the popup, complete your purchase via the Flying Blue link, and miles are credited to your account automatically.

FAQ
Common questions
When will the extension be available?

We're currently building and testing it. Sign up via the "Notify me" button above and you'll get an email as soon as it's live on the Chrome Web Store.

Will it work on Firefox or Safari?

The first release will be Chrome only. Firefox and Edge support are planned for a later version. Safari is unlikely due to technical limitations.

Does it track my browsing?

No. The extension only activates on known Flying Blue partner domains. It does not log your activity, send data to any server, or store your browsing history.

Is it free?

Yes, completely free. Like Miles Optimizer itself, the extension is an independent open source project with no ads or paid tiers.

How many partners does it recognize?

At launch it will cover all 300+ retailers from Earn Online and the full Shop for Miles catalogue — automatically updated daily from the same data that powers this website.
